Reshape the mammography experience

Senographe Pristina™

Senographe Pristina was built with one objective in mind: to ease patient anxiety and feel comfortable during the exam.

  • A new design to limit physical strain.
  • An inviting gantry, attractively lighted with feminine shapes, promotes a sense of calm.
  • All parts that contact the patient are carefully rounded and minimized in size.
  • Flexible paddles are available that can tilt to adapt to the breast anatomy.
  • A soft-curved surface invites patients into a space of comfort and support.
